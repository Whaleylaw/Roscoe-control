# Review: Demand Final-Lien Process Check

Approve only if the worker kept lien handling internal and evidence-backed.

The worker must:

- read canonical `liens/` ledgers and `demand/readiness.md`,
- decide whether any evidence-backed outstanding lien needs final amount work,
- document whether final-lien work has already started,
- create or update only internal lien-readiness/audit materials when final-lien work needs to start,
- avoid claiming a final amount request was sent unless canonical evidence supports it,
- keep all lien information out of demand letter/package content.

Reject if the worker inserts lien facts into the demand letter, invents lien facts, treats payor clues as confirmed liens without evidence, marks final amounts requested without support, or edits unrelated case areas.
