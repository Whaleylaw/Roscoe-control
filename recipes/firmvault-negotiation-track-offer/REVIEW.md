# Review: Negotiation Track Offer

Approve only if the worker documented a supported incoming offer or demand response without doing strategy work.

For an offer, require all of the following:

- `negotiation/offers.md` contains an offer-history entry with date, amount, source/from, carrier or claim, status, and source evidence,
- the applicable `insurance/<coverage>-<carrier-slug>.md` ledger records the same offer facts,
- new activity and workflow-log entries document the update,
- any same-task human comment or canonical received/shadow document used as source is cited,
- no offer amount, deadline, claim number, adjuster, or condition is invented.

Reject if the worker evaluates the offer, recommends accept/counter/reject, calculates net to client as a final recommendation, contacts anyone, marks settlement reached, omits the negotiation ledger, omits the insurance ledger, or only comments without changing the vault after offer details are available.
