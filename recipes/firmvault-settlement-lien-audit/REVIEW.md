# Review: Settlement Lien Audit

Approve only if the worker made an evidence-backed lien determination and preserved the pre-distribution boundary.

The worker must:

- read canonical `liens/`, settlement, distribution, provider/payor, demand, negotiation, and insurance evidence,
- create or update `liens/settlement-lien-audit.md`,
- either document no outstanding settlement liens or classify/prioritize supported liens,
- preserve TBD values instead of inventing fee, cost, final amount, or payment facts,
- append activity and workflow-log entries.

Reject if the worker creates speculative liens, treats medical treatment alone as a lien, invents final amounts or reductions, sends/claims to send external communications, pays liens, or marks final distribution complete.
