# Review: Final Distribution Completion

Approve only if the worker documented final distribution from explicit human/test confirmation.

The worker must:

- rely on same-task comments for issuance, receipt, and final trust-balance facts,
- update `documents/sent/settlement/final-distribution-sent.md`,
- update settlement/distribution ledgers to reflect final distribution completion,
- append activity and workflow-log entries.

Reject if the worker invents payment or receipt facts, marks trust zero without explicit confirmation, leaves unresolved liens/holdbacks unaddressed, or edits old logs.
