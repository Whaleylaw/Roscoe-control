# Review: Client Check-In Cadence

Approve only if the worker established or verified the check-in cadence without pretending to contact the client.

Approve when:

- `client/check-ins.md` records the cadence state or points to existing cadence evidence.
- `client/contactability.md` is updated only with supported contactability facts.
- Next check-in timing is recorded as a workflow/cadence fact, not as proof of contact.
- Activity and workflow-log entries are append-only.

Reject if the worker sends or claims to send a client communication, invents contact preferences, invents treatment updates, or creates duplicate cadence records.
