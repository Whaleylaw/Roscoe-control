# Review: Demand Readiness

Approve only if `demand/readiness.md` is evidence-backed and limited to demand-readiness work.

The worker must:

- use canonical FirmVault paths instead of broad document searching,
- summarize provider records, bills, chronology, insurance, accident/liability status, and internal lien-process readiness,
- calculate only supported medical specials and paid/unpaid amounts,
- clearly list missing items or blockers,
- add activity and workflow-log entries,
- avoid drafting the demand letter, disclosing lien details in demand content, or making unsupported valuation recommendations.

Reject if the worker invents damages, policy limits, liens, provider facts, claim status, suggests lien details belong in the demand letter, or changes unrelated case areas.
