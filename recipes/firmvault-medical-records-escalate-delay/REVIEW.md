# Review: Medical Records Delay Escalation

Approve only if the escalation is supported by the prior request/follow-up history and gives a specific next action.

Check:

- Original request and follow-ups are documented.
- Missing records/bills are identified precisely.
- Escalation recommendation matches the delay reason.
- Attorney/human review is requested where legal judgment or external contact is needed.
- No unsupported facts or external communications were added.

Reject if the worker escalates without checking current receipt status, fabricates provider responses, or changes unrelated case files.
