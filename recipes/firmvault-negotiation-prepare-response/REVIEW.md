# Review: Negotiation Response Preparation

Approve only if the worker prepared a human-send handoff from supported decision evidence and did not take external action.

The worker must:

- identify the current supported client decision from canonical negotiation files or insurance ledgers,
- prepare the correct response handoff and generated letter under `documents/generated/insurance/`,
- include carrier/adjuster, claim number, offer amount, decision authority, and missing send/release instructions where applicable,
- append new activity and workflow-log entries,
- leave all external communication to the human-send gate,
- avoid marking settlement reached, counter sent, rejected, or impasse at this stage.

Reject if the worker invents decision facts, sends or claims to send a response, marks settlement reached, starts settlement processing, edits old logs, or omits the generated handoff/letter when facts are sufficient.
