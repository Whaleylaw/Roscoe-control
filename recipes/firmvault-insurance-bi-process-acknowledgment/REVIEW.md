# Review: BI Acknowledgment Processing

Approve only if the worker correctly distinguishes one of these outcomes:

- Acknowledgment arrived and all claim/adjuster/contact details are supported by vault evidence or owner confirmation.
- No acknowledgment arrived and a human-ready follow-up instruction or draft was prepared without claiming external contact.
- Mail was returned/defective and the task was routed to Human Review with the exact correction needed.

Reject if the worker invents claim, adjuster, liability, coverage, or contact details; marks acknowledgment complete without evidence; sends or claims to send a follow-up; skips the BI ledger; or fails to leave activity/workflow-log audit evidence.
