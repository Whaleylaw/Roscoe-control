# Review: Client Check-In Handoff

Approve only if the worker prepared a usable human handoff without sending or claiming client contact.

Approve when:

- The handoff is tailored to current case facts, providers, treatment status, and outstanding items.
- Missing facts are marked unknown instead of invented.
- The output makes clear that a human still needs to contact the client.
- Activity and workflow-log entries are append-only.

Reject if the worker says the client was contacted, sends or drafts as if sent, invents client responses, or buries the handoff outside canonical client check-in/activity paths.
