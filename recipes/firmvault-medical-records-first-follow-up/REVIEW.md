# Review: First Medical Records Follow-Up

Approve only if the worker checked for received records/bills before preparing follow-up and documented the status clearly.

Check:

- Original request date/provider is identified.
- Received records or bills were checked first.
- Pending items are specific.
- Human follow-up handoff is actionable.
- No external call, fax, email, or portal action was performed.

Reject if the worker skipped the receipt check, guessed the provider status, or changed unrelated files.
